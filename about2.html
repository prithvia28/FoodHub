Who are we?

Launched in Mumbai in 2019, Food Hub has grown from a collge project to one of the largest food aggregators in the Mumbai. We cater food to 20+ companies/colleges in Mumbai, enabling our vision of better food for more people. We not only connect people to food in every context but work closely with restaurants to enable a sustainable ecosystem.

Our Goals

Improving quality of food
We are committed to nurturing a neutral platform and are helping food establishments maintain high standards through Hyperpure. Food Hygiene Ratings is a coveted mark of quality among our restaurant partners.

Focusing on affordability
The benefits of providing an interesting choice are embodied by the success of Zomato Gold. We are leaving no stone unturned when it comes to making food more affordable without compromising on the profitability of a given restaurant..

Boosting accessibility for customers
Our delivery service is reaching more and more cities. We are actively growing our services - table reservation, food@work for corporate catering and are continuing to power Feeding India.

Our Values

Resilience
We push ourselves beyond our abilities when faced with tough times. When we foresee uncertainty, we address it only with flexibility.

Acceptance
Feedback is never taken personally, we break it into positive pieces and strive to work on each and every element even more effectively.

Ownership
People here don’t work ‘for’ Food Hub, they work ‘with’ Food Hub. We treat every problem as our own, take accountability and drive the change.

Spark
We believe in, stand for and are evangelists of our culture - both, within Food Hub and externally with all our stakeholders

Humility
It’s always ‘us’ over ‘me’. We don’t lose ourselves in pride or confidence during individual successes, but focus on being our simple selves in every which way

Judgement
It’s not our abilities that show who we truly are - it’s our choices. We aim to get these right, at least in the majority of the cases.
